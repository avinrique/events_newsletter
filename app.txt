🔹 Refined Story (with your corrections)

SuperAdmin
	•	What they do:
	•	Create Departments (CSE, ECE, etc.).
	•	Define Designations for Teachers only (Professor, Asst. Prof., HOD, etc.).
	•	Create Admin accounts.
	•	What they don’t do:
	•	No role in clubs, events, reports, student designations.
	•	Doesn’t manage Presidents, VPs, or club hierarchies.

⸻

Admin
	•	What they do:
	•	Create users: HODs, Teachers, Students.
	•	Assign designations (teachers only).
	•	Assign department for each HOD, teacher, student.
	•	View all department reports (generated by HOD).
	•	View budgets approved by HOD.
	•	What they don’t do:
	•	No role in creating clubs.
	•	No direct event approvals.

⸻

HOD
	•	What they do:
	•	Approve clubs created by teachers.
	•	Approve events (whether teacher-driven or club-driven).
	•	Approve budgets for events & projects.
	•	Generate department report (consolidating projects, internships, certificates, events, clubs).
	•	Scope: only within their department.

⸻

Teacher
	•	What they do:
	•	Create clubs (propose to HOD).
	•	Act as club mentors.
	•	Create events:
	•	Personal Teacher Event (single teacher).
	•	Joint Teacher Event (multiple teachers).
	•	Club Event (as mentor/initiator).
	•	Create Projects:
	•	Teacher-only project (solo or joint with other teachers).
	•	Teacher + Student project (mentored project).
	•	Upload own certificates (FDP, conferences, MOOCs, etc.).
	•	Manage student data:
	•	Add projects, internships, certificates on behalf of students.
	•	Approve/reject student-submitted items.
	•	Special note: If a teacher uploads something for a student, it is auto-approved.

⸻

Student
	•	What they do:
	•	Maintain personal profile: USN/temp USN, roll, semester, dept, image, contact info.
	•	Upload:
	•	Projects (need teacher approval unless added by teacher).
	•	Internships (need teacher approval unless added by teacher).
	•	Certificates (need teacher approval unless added by teacher).
	•	Join clubs; may hold a club role (President/VP/etc., only inside club).
	•	Participate in events/projects.

⸻

Clubs
	•	Created by Teachers → must be approved by HOD.
	•	Clubs have:
	•	Name, purpose, department.
	•	Flexible structure (President, VP, Secretary, etc.).
	•	Members (students with roles).
	•	Mentor teachers.
	•	Events list.
	•	Reports: Clubs have their own activity reports (events, participants, budgets, outcomes).

⸻

Events
	•	Can be organized by:
	1.	Club (club event → mentor approval → HOD approval).
	2.	Teacher (personal teacher event → HOD approval).
	3.	Multiple Teachers (joint teacher event → HOD approval).
	•	Events include:
	•	Title, type, description.
	•	Organizers (teacher(s) and/or club).
	•	Participants (students, teachers, external if needed).
	•	Budget (requested, approved by HOD, utilized).
	•	Dates, venue.
	•	Outcomes & evidence (attendance, photos, reports).

⸻

Projects
	•	Types:
	•	Student projects (mini, major, personal → require teacher approval).
	•	Teacher-only projects (solo research or with other teachers).
	•	Teacher + Student projects (joint work).
	•	Each project may have:
	•	Title, type, domain, abstract.
	•	Students, teachers involved.
	•	Budget (requested, approved).
	•	Outcome (prototype, paper, product, patent).
	•	Evidence files.

⸻

Certificates
	•	Belong to: students or teachers.
	•	Types: NPTEL, Udemy, Coursera, Hackathon (participation/winner), FDP, others.
	•	Uploaded with: title, issuer, category (completion/participation/winner), date, file/link.
	•	Approval flow:
	•	If student uploads → teacher approves.
	•	If teacher uploads (own or on behalf of student) → auto-approved.

⸻

Internships
	•	Belong to students.
	•	Fields: company, role, offer letter, joining date, leaving date, stipend, mentor teacher.
	•	Approval flow same as certificates.

⸻

🔹 Entities (clean list)
	1.	Users (superadmin, admin, hod, teacher, student).
	2.	Departments.
	3.	Designations (for teachers only).
	4.	Clubs (with structure, mentors, members).
	5.	Events (club/teacher/joint teacher).
	6.	Projects (student, teacher, joint).
	7.	Certificates (student, teacher).
	8.	Internships (student).
	9.	Reports (student, teacher, club, department).
	10.	Budgets (linked to events/projects).

⸻

🔹 Flow (summary)
	1.	SuperAdmin → create dept, designations → create admin.
	2.	Admin → create HOD, Teachers, Students (assign dept/designation).
	3.	Teacher →
	•	Create clubs (→ HOD approves).
	•	Create projects/events (solo/joint/student/club).
	•	Upload certificates (self or student).
	•	Approve student submissions.
	4.	Student → upload projects, internships, certificates → teacher approves.
	5.	Clubs → run events (mentor approves → HOD approves).
	6.	HOD → approves clubs, events, budgets → generates dept reports.
	7.	Admin → views dept reports & budgets.
	8.	Reports auto-generate at different levels.
